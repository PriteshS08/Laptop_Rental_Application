We are introducing this Laptop Rental Application, through that anyone can rent the product for their uses during such a pandemic as a corona. In this proposed solution there will be two modules (i.e) customer and lender. The lender can post their products and they can accept the rental request from the customer. Customers can search for the laptop with the required specifications and can send a request to the lender. The lender and customer can make an agreement at a specific place nearby them and the customer can get the product directly from the lender.
